Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CreatorSync

CreatorSync is a comprehensive platform for music producers to monetize their beats, collaborate with artists in real-time, and grow their music business. We provide marketplace features, professional audio tools like The Finisher and MixMaster1, and powerful collaboration capabilities.

CreatorSync offers multiple tiers: Starter ($9.99/month or $7.99/month yearly), Pro ($29.99/month or $23.99/month yearly) with MixMaster1 access, and Enterprise ($99.99/month or $79.99/month yearly) with all premium features. All plans include a 15-day free trial, then 50% off the first month, followed by full pricing.

CreatorSync takes a 12.5% commission on all beat sales. This covers payment processing, platform maintenance, and ongoing feature development. You keep 87.5% of every sale.

Standard pricing lets you set a fixed price for your beat. Bidding allows buyers to place bids, with a minimum bid price you set. Bidding beats can be marked as "trending" to increase visibility. Bidding typically results in higher final sale prices for in-demand beats.

MixMaster1 is our professional audio mixing suite featuring multi-track editing, advanced EQ, effects, and high-quality audio export. It's exclusively available to Pro and Enterprise subscribers. The tool integrates seamlessly with The Finisher for complete music production workflow.

Yes! CreatorSync offers real-time collaboration features including live project sharing, synchronized editing, real-time chat, version control, and secure file encryption. Multiple users can work on the same project simultaneously from anywhere in the world.

We support all major audio formats including WAV, MP3, FLAC, AIFF, OGG, and AAC. For best quality, we recommend uploading WAV or FLAC files. Our system automatically generates multiple format versions for streaming and download.

Every beat has a share button that opens our ShareManager. You can instantly share to 13+ platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, WhatsApp, Telegram, and more. Track share analytics in your dashboard.

Yes! All subscription tiers include a 15-day free trial. A credit/debit card is required to start your trial. After the trial, you'll be charged 50% off for the first month, then full price thereafter. Cancel anytime during the trial period with no charges.

CreatorSync is available in 7 languages: English, Spanish (Español), French (Français), German (Deutsch), Japanese (日本語), Chinese (中文), and Russian (Русский). Switch languages anytime using the selector in the navigation bar.

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Cash App, Chime, and One Card. All payments are processed securely through Stripe with enterprise-grade encryption.
